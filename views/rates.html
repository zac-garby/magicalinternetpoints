what do these mean?

for some point source (e.g. stars), there are the classes into which points can fall: low, medium, and high.

if low is 50, for example, the first 50 of your "raw" points for that source (e.g. the first 50 stars) will be multiplied by the corresponding low rate for that source.

if, then, medium was 150, the next 100 of your stars would be multiplied by the corresponding medium rate, and so on.

the high rate applies to any raw points for a source exceeding the medium cap.
